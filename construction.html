Newly Constructed Homes

Did you purchase a newly constructed home and your builder’s warranty is about to expire? Despite the fact that you may have had a home inspection performed prior to purchasing your new home, there can be issues that arise which were not present on the day of inspection. Homes sometimes require to be lived in and mechanicals need to be used for a period of time before defects can show their ugly face. A pre-warranty expiration inspection can be performed to avoid having to pay to repair these defects out of your own pockets. It also goes without saying that a “peace of mind” is priceless.
