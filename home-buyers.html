“Make the Right Decision”

Are you in the market or purchasing a new home? A home inspection of a property is always highly recommended prior to purchase. A home may look to be in great condition at first glance, but after looking deeply into the exterior, mechanicals, conditioned and unconditioned spaces of a property there may be deficiencies that arise. I advise my clients not to be deterred from purchasing a home based off defects found, as all homes will have them in some capacity. Some defects will carry greater safety concerns than others. My goal is to give the most information I can during an inspection, whether positive or negative, which will in turn put my clients on the Right Path for them and their family.
